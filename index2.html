2. History

For over 40 years, Libero System has made great strides in manufacturing leather goods and business planners for customers worldwide.
Our commitment to our customers in delivering products that meet and exceed expectations has been the cornerstone of our success.

However, we are in constant search of new opportunities to create products that resonate with customers.
In this pandemic, we decided to address the changing market's challenges by pursuing new possibilities for our future.
Our ability to respond rapidly to changes without losing momentum has been an integral part of our growth.
With Libero Mask's launch, we have expanded our business to produce high quality medical and non-medical masks and face coverings.
In addition to face masks, Libero Mask plans to introduce respirators, surgical gowns, and a host of other personal protective equipment shortly.

Libero Mask is proud to take part in the global effort to safeguard public health.
We promise to deliver safe and effective products to our customers all around the world.
