Libero Cotton Comfy Organic Cotton Layer Skin-friendly Adjustable Strap Cotton 2-ply Extra Soft Breathable Wash Reuse Replaceable Filters GOTS Certified Lining Eco-friendly Face Mask

1. Libero Cotton Comfy Mask features 100% organic cotton inner layer certified by the Global Organic Textile Standard (GOTS), the world's leading processing standard for textiles made from organic fibers.


2. Extra soft, skin-friendly texture and superior breathability. Wash, reuse, and produce less waste.


3. Filter pocket allows SMS filters to be inserted for extra protection. Each mask comes with 2 replaceable SMS filters. SMS (Spunbond Meltblown Spunbond) filters are professional quality filtering inserts offering excellent barrier qualities against particles and droplets.


4. Adjustable ear strap for customized fit without behind-the-ear discomfort. Removable elastic back strap for additional tension relief around the ears. Allows the mask to be hung around the neck while not in use.


5. Care Instructions: Hand wash in lukewarm water using mild detergent or soap. Do not bleach. Do not iron. Air dry.



Product Description:
This mask hits all the right notes: comfortable, washable, and breathable.
It features a pure cotton exterior and 100% organic cotton inner layer certified by the Global Organic Textile Standard (GOTS), the world's leading processing standard for textiles made from organic fibers. The mask comes in four easy-to-match color options that will complement almost any outfit. It also features a built-in pocket for filter inserts as well as adjustable ear loops to help you find the perfect fit and avoid behind-the-ear discomfort.
Sizing:
M – 7.87" W* x 5.70" H (20 x 14.5 cm)
L – 8.26" W* x 6.10" H (21 x 15.5 cm)
*Width (measured side-to-side, not including ear loops)

Material Composition:

Outer layer - 100% Cotton
Inner layer - 100% Organic Cotton
Loop - 86% Nylon + 14% Elastane
Adjuster - Silicone

Color Options: Black, Charcoal Gray, Light Blue Dots, Light Gray Dots
